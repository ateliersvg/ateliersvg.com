About Atelier SVG

Atelier SVG is an open-source PHP library for parsing, manipulating, optimizing, and morphing SVG documents, written in pure PHP, zero runtime dependencies, distributed under the MIT license.

#Maintainer

Atelier SVG is created and maintained by Simon André, a French open-source developer. Find more of his work on his personal site and on GitHub.
